I.                Information Wrainbo Collects

A.              Personal Information and Non-Identifying Information about End Users

If you create an account with us to use the Platform as an End User, we may ask you for personally identifiable information. This refers to information about you that can be used to contact or identify you (Personal Information). Personal Information includes, but is not limited to, your full name, email address, and any other information that could be used to personally identify you.

We also collect other information that you provide as part of registration and the administration and personalization of your account with us that does not identify you (Non-Identifying Information). Non-Identifying Information includes, but is not limited to, your zip code (on its own) and individual preferences. Certain Non-Identifying Information would be considered a part of your Personal Information if it were combined with other identifiers (for example, combining your zip code with your street address) in a way that enables you to be identified. But the same pieces of information are considered Non-Identifying Information when they are taken alone or combined only with other Non-Identifying Information (for example, your viewing preferences).

As detailed further below, we use your Personal Information (in some cases, in conjunction with your Non-Identifying Information) mainly to operate the Platform, to facilitate and complete transactions for you, and to respond to correspondence from you.

We may also combine your Personal Information with Non-Identifying Information and we may aggregate your information with information collected from other End Users.

B.              Usage Data

The Platform collects information from your mobile or computing device in connection with your access to and use of the Platform that we refer to as Usage Data. Usage Data includes, for example, the manufacturer and model of your device, your Internet Service Provider (ISP); your devices Internet Protocol (IP) address (or other device identifier), browser type, and operating system; referring/exit pages; clickstream data; pages of the Website that you visit, the time spent on those pages or interacting with certain portions of the mobile application, information you search for on the Platform; Website and mobile application access times and dates; and other statistics.

Usage Data may include geolocation data if, for example, you use a third-party service account to access the Platform and such third-party service account provides geolocation data. The Platform will request your express permission to collect geolocation data from your mobile device if you use a mobile application to access the Platform.

Usage Data may be non-identifying or it may be associated with you. We may also combine Usage Data we collect from you with other information provided by you or that we receive from other sources. Whenever we associate Usage Data with Personal Information, we will treat it as Personal Information.

II.             How Wrainbo Collects Information

Wrainbo may use a number of different techniques and technologies to collect Personal Information, Non-Identifying Information, and Usage Data when you access and use the Platform.

A.              Forms

We request certain information from you through the use of forms on a Websites and in a mobile application, such as when you register or create an account or when you send us correspondence through the Website.

B.              Web Browser and Mobile Application

Usage Data are collected from the computing or mobile device you use when you access the Platform through a Website using Web browser software or by using a mobile application.

C.              Cookies

As is regular practice on many websites, Wrainbo Websites use cookies and other technologies to collect information about how the Platform is being used.  A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to and stored on your device from our server.  Cookies may, for example, allow a user to enter a password less frequently or remember a users preferences during an online session.

Our Websites primarily use cookies to facilitate Website content loading and distribution.  We also use cookies to track Web analytics, such as page and content views, so that we can enhance your experience on the Websites.  Our Websites include first-party cookies (i.e., cookies that send data directly to Wrainbo and agents and contractors acting on behalf of Wrainbo, including cookies used to monitor, analyze, and administer the Platform). Our Websites do not include third-party cookies (i.e., cookies that send data to our contractors for their commercial use), and the cookies on our Platform do not track Internet activity across different websites.

Since cookies are now used as industry standard, most devices automatically accept cookies, but you can usually change your devices settings to decline them.  If you prefer not to enable cookies, you may choose to disable them; however, please note that certain features on the Platform will not be available to you once cookies are disabled.

D.              Click-through URLS

In some communications to End Users, Wrainbo may use a click-through URL linked to content on the Platform. When an End User clicks onto one of these URLs, the End User will pass through our server before arriving at the destination Web page. Wrainbo tracks this click-through data to help us determine End User interest in certain subject matter and measure the effectiveness of these End User communications. You can avoid being tracked in this way by not clicking text or graphic links in emails from Wrainbo.

E.              Tracking

Some Web browsers may be configured to send Do Not Track signals to websites, or Internet users may use similar mechanisms, to indicate the users preference that certain web technologies not be used to track the users online activity. The Platform does not accept or process such Do Not Track signals or similar mechanisms.

F.               Public Communications

Please note that if you use any bulletin board, chat room, comment posting feature, or other public communication service, forum, or feature offered through the Platform, or post any information available for viewing by other End Users, any of the information that you share will be visible to other End Users. The information that you make available can be read, used, and collected by other End Users to send you unsolicited messages outside of the Platform. Wrainbo is not responsible for the manner in which the Personal Information that you decide to share using a public communication service will be used by other End Users.
